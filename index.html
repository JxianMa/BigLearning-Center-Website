Welcome to Big Learning Center

NSF I/UCRC Center for Big Learning (BigLearning) is to create a multi-site multi-disciplinary center that explores research frontiers in emerging large-scale deep learning and machine learning for a broad spectrum of big data applications, designs novel cloud services and big data ecosystems to enable big learning research and applications, transfers research discoveries to meet urgent needs in industry with our diverse center members, and nurtures our next generation talents in a mixed forward-looking setting with real-world relevance and significance by the industry-university consortium.
